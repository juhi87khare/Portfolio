Introduction
About Me

I'm interested in one simple question: why do complex systems like healthcare still struggle to make good decisions even with so much data?

During my time at Banner Health, I saw firsthand that the challenge isn't just technology. It's how decisions are made across fragmented systems, workflows, and stakeholders. That experience pushed me to explore how product thinking, analytics, and emerging AI can be used to build systems that don't just report what's happening, but help teams understand why it's happening, what could happen next, and what they should do about it.

Currently, I'm pursuing my Master's in Information Management at UIUC, where I focus on data-driven decision-making, product strategy, and healthcare systems. I'm particularly interested in building decision intelligence systems that improve operational efficiency, reduce friction in workflows, and make healthcare delivery more responsive and scalable.
